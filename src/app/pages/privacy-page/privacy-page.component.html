Privacy & Data Handling

QueryLens runs locally in your browser. Your datasets stay on your device and are never transmitted to QueryLens servers.

What is stored locally

Imported files, fetched API responses, query history, and workspace preferences in this browser profile.

What is not collected

No account identifiers, no record payload uploads, no non-essential cookies, and no tracking scripts.

Your responsibility

You must be authorized to process the data you load and comply with your organization’s data policies.
